# PLATFORM SLOGAN BANK

## 中文
- 不是更多 agent，是更完整的组织。
- 不是换皮，是让同一个 AI 内核长进不同世界。
- 一个内核，很多世界；一个组织，很多主题。
- 今天是三省六部，明天是西游、后宫、仙班。
- 让 AI 不止会回答，还会按组织结构上班。
- 从多 agent，到多世界里的组织系统。

## English
- Not more agents. Better organization.
- Not just skins — one AI kernel growing across many worlds.
- One kernel, many worlds. One organization, many themes.
- Today 三省六部. Tomorrow Journey to the West, palace worlds, and celestial courts.
- Make AI do more than answer. Make it work inside an organization.
- From multi-agent to multi-world organization systems.
