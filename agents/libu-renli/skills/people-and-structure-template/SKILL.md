---
name: people-and-structure-template
description: Evaluate role fit, org structure, and staffing decisions for 吏部 style governance.
---

Use this skill when deciding whether a role should exist, change, merge, or be removed.

Default output format:

```md
# 吏部编制单
- Role / Position Under Review:
- Structural Need:
- Current Problem:
- Recommended Change:
- Expected Benefit:
- Governance Notes:
```

Rules:
- Focus on role fit and structural clarity.
- Avoid adding roles without a clear need.
- Prefer cleaner structure over ornamental complexity.
