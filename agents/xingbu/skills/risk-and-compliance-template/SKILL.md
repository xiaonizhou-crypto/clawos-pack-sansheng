---
name: risk-and-compliance-template
description: Surface boundary, safety, and downside concerns for 刑部 style risk review.
---

Use this skill when a task needs a sharper boundary or explicit risk framing.

Default output format:

```md
# 刑部风控单
- Action Under Review:
- Boundary Concern:
- Risk Level: low | medium | high
- Failure Mode:
- Safe Alternative:
- Recommendation:
```

Rules:
- Name the real downside, not vague caution.
- Distinguish legal/ethical/operational risks when relevant.
- Do not block action without explaining why.
